Frequently asked questions

When can I redeem the complimentary CapCut Pro membership?
The redemption period runs from June 30, 2024 at 00:00 AM, GMT+8 and ends on Mar 31, 2027 at 11:59 PM, GMT+8.
Which ASUS products qualify for the promotion?
You can see a complete list of eligible ASUS products on the promotion website’s Eligible Products section. However, here’s a quick summary: ProArt laptops: 6-month subscription; Selected models of ROG, TUF, and Vivobook laptops: 1-month subscription.
How do I claim my free CapCut membership with an eligible PC purchase?
Simply register a new account via the pre-installed CapCut app in the Start menu of your new ASUS PC during the promotional period.
Do I need to provide payment details to redeem the offer?
No, you won’t need to provide any payment details to redeem your complimentary membership.
In which regions is this promotion available?
The promotion is currently available in the regions listed below, with subscription services as shown in below. CapCut Pro is expected to expand to additional markets, enabling redemption in those regions in the future.
The Asia-Pacific: Australia, Bahrain, Brunei, Cambodia, China, Cyprus, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Israel, Japan, Kuwait, Laos, Macau, Malaysia, Myanmar, New Zealand, Oman, the Philippines, Palau, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, the United Arab Emirates, and Vietnam. The America region: Anguilla, Antigua and Barbuda, Argentina, Aruba, the Bahamas, Barbados, Bermuda, Brazil, the British Virgin Islands, Canada, the Cayman Islands, Chile, Colombia, Curaçao, Ecuador, Guyana, Mexico, Montserrat, Panama, Peru, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Trinidad and Tobago, Turks and Caicos Islands, the United States, Uruguay, and Venezuela. The Europe region: Austria, Belgium, Croatia, the Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, San Marino, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and Vatican City. The Africa region: Seychelles.
If I receive the membership on my PC, can I use it on other devices?
Yes, the ASUS membership is multi-platform, giving you access via PC, mobile (including iPadOS), and web.
Do I need a new CapCut account to qualify for the offer if I already have one?
Yes, you must register a new CapCut account to redeem the complimentary membership.
Can I still participate in the promotion if I already have a CapCut Pro subscription?
Yes, you are still eligible for the promotion. However, you’ll need to create a new CapCut account instead of adding it to your existing subscription.
What happens if I activate and use the membership for one month but do not continue to use it in the following months?
Your complimentary membership quota will be retained until you log in again. You will only use the membership benefits when you are actively using CapCut Pro on a monthly basis.
What happens after the six-month or one-month subscription ends?
After the free period ends, you can purchase a subscription at the current price to continue using CapCut Pro. If you don’t renew, your subscription will automatically cancel.
