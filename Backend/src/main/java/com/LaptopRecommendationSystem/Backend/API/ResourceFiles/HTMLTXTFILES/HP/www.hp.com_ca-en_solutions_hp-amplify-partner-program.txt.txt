about HP AMPLIFY Apply to become an HP Partner Locate an HP reseller Visit the HP Partner Portal HP PARTNER PROGRAM A powerful global1 partner program designed for the customer-driven digital age. A simplified and easy-to-navigate global1 structure with two distinct tracks Rewards partners for performance, collaboration, and capabilities Insight-driven data and collaboration tools to accelerate digital transformation Learn Three core pillars focused on driving greater partner efficiency, insight, and profitability Performance Earn rewards for a variety of performance indicators tied to portfolio sell-thru revenue metrics. Capabilities Hone digital skills and provide a more consistent customer experience across multiple channels. Together, we’ll harness this data to produce valuable new insights and enhance the customer journey. Collaboration Invest in improving capabilities to become more competitive, relevant, and ultimately, more profitable in areas such as data sharing, e-commerce, solutions and managed services capabilities.
